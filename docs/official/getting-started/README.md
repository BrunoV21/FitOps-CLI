# Getting Started

Everything you need to go from zero to your first training insights.

## Steps

1. [Installation](./installation.md) — Install FitOps-CLI
2. [Authentication](./authentication.md) — Connect your Strava account
3. [First Sync](./first-sync.md) — Download your activities

## After Setup

Once you've synced, you're ready to explore:

- [`fitops activities list`](../commands/activities.md) — Browse your activities
- [`fitops analytics training-load`](../commands/analytics.md) — See your fitness trend
- [`fitops athlete profile`](../commands/athlete.md) — Your profile and gear
