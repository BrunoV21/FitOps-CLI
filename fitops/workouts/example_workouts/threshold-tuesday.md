## Warmup
10 min easy (Z1–Z2)

## Main Set
4 × 8 min @ Z4 (LT2 pace / lactate threshold HR)
2 min Z1 recovery jog between each rep

## Cooldown
8 min easy (Z1)

---

**Goal:** Improve lactate threshold pace and HR efficiency.
**RPE Target:** 6–7/10 during intervals.
**Key metric:** avg HR during intervals should be 95–99% LTHR.
