## The Run
90 min continuous at Z2 (aerobic base / conversational pace).
Avg HR should stay below LT1 (< 92% LTHR) throughout.

Last 15 min: optionally pick up to marathon pace (Z3) if feeling good.

---

**Goal:** Build aerobic base, fat oxidation, and mileage durability.
**RPE Target:** 4–5/10 — you should be able to hold a full conversation.
**Key metric:** avg HR over full run, and any HR drift in the final 30 min.
